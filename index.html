About Us

Coffee within the twin island of Trinidad & Tobago is of pristine quality and unrecognized internationally due to low export quantites and local consumption.

Our main mission at Filters Coffeehouse is global dominance within the brewing business and to bring awareness to the quality of coffee that is brewed in Trinidad and Tobago.

Filters hopes to bring joy, energy and production to the entire Caribbean population through our coffee.
